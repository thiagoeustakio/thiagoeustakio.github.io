Haptics and Robots Research Group

Welcome to HRRG!

The Haptics and Robots Research Group (HRRG) at Lakehead University is working on enhancing haptics and tactile capabilities for robots, as well as engineering these robots to perform tasks in industry and everyday applications.

Graduate Students

Interested in joining us?

Applicants should fulfill the following requirements: (1) Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, or Electrical and Computer Engineering; (2) High GPA; (3) Good programming and mathematical analysis/optimization skills; and (4) Good IELTS score (given the candidate has already taken the test).

Interested applicants must send a brief CV and Transcripts to thiago.oliveira (at) lakeheadu.ca, failure to submit both defines the candidate as not being serious. Potential candidates will be contacted for interviews over Zoom, particularly to verifying domain knowledge in undergraduate project/thesis and programming skills.

Experience with the following skills and tools is considered a bonus.
